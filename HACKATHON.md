## Idea: osint.market
A decentralized marketplace for OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) research:
- Agents and humans can request investigations
- OSINT specialists compete to fulfill bounties
- All payments and reputation tracked on-chain via Solana
- Bounty escrow via Solana PDAs
- Payments in USDC/SOL
- On-chain reputation scores
- NFT badges for completed investigations
- Solana Pay integration for instant settlement
